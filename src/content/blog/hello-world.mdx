Writing is how I solidify what I learn. Reading and watching only gets you so far — the moment you try to explain something clearly, you find out what you actually understand versus what you just recognised.

## What I'm learning

Right now I'm working through Anthropic's courses, participating in Dicoding's hackathon, and building real projects through my agency. Each one of these is a source of posts here.

## What to expect

Some posts will be structured notes — "here's what I learned about X." Others will be more personal — what it felt like, what went wrong, what surprised me.

Either way, everything here is genuine. Thanks for reading.
